OUR VISION

At The Dream Canvas Education Centre, our mission is to empower individuals to become integral parts of the vibrant hospitality industry. Through comprehensive training in hospitality management, we aim to cultivate globally competitive managers and professionals who are prepared to excel in this dynamic field. We are committed to canvassing and nurturing the hospitality manpower of tomorrow.

OUR MISSION

Our Vision at The Dream Canvas is to pioneer the advancement of hospitality professionals by providing comprehensive skills development, immersive industry exposure, and strategic leadership training. We are committed to empowering our students with the knowledge, skills, and experience necessary to excel in managerial roles within the dynamic hotel, tourism, and hospitality sectors. As a leading hospitality management institute, we aspire to cultivate a new generation of industry leaders through rigorous education and close collaboration with the sector to address the demand for highly skilled professionals.

OUR GOAL

Our goal at The Dream Canvas Education Centre is to develop world-class professionals by staying attuned to the evolving trends and requirements of the hospitality sector. Through our programs, we aim to empower students to realize their aspirations and acquire the comprehensive knowledge needed to emerge as leaders, executives, operatives, and entrepreneurs in the dynamic realm of hospitality.

Roopali Dame

(CEO & Founder)

As the Founder of The Dream Canvas Hospitality organization, she brings a wealth of experience in education and the hotel industry. Her strategic outlook and academic expertise have been honed through years of experience in the Hospitality and Tourism education sector. Passionate about transforming the hospitality landscape, Roopali is a visionary leader driving excellence in education and industry practices.

Ishani Pundlik

(Centre Head)

Ishani Pundlik is a dynamic figure in the hospitality industry, possessing a wealth of experience in bartending education, hotel industry recruitment, and organizational management. Her roles as a dedicated faculty member in bartending programs and a recruiter in the hotel industry highlight her commitment to shaping the future of hospitality professionals and connecting talent with rewarding opportunities.
