CERTIFICATE IN PROFESSIONAL BARTENDING

Certificate in Professional Bartending is a 3-month program focused on honing bartending skills. Participants receive hands-on training in crafting classic cocktails and mocktails, along with instruction in flaring techniques. The course includes live event exposure and industry visits, preparing students for real-world scenarios. Additionally, an internship with comprehensive placement assistance ensures smooth entry into professional roles. Graduates emerge with refined skills and industry connections, ready to excel in bartending careers.

Eligibility Criterion: 10th & above

Duration: 3 months

Assessment scheme: Weekly assessment of theory and practical

Internship: Live training event exposure giving chance to earn while you learn program.
